# 📊 Retail Sales Performance Dashboard – 2025

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a dynamic **Retail Sales Performance Dashboard** built in Microsoft Excel to analyze sales, profitability, regional performance, and product trends for the year 2025.

The dashboard is designed in a corporate MIS reporting style and enables interactive business analysis using slicers and pivot-based visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor overall revenue and profitability
- Track performance against sales targets
- Identify top-performing regions and products
- Analyze category contribution
- Detect seasonal sales trends
- Enable interactive filtering for deeper insights

---

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers (Region, Category, Product, Date)
- Combo Charts with Secondary Axis
- KPI calculations using advanced formulas
- Clean MIS-style formatting

---

## 📂 Dataset Details

The dataset contains retail transactional data including:

- Order ID  
- Order Date  
- Region (East, West, North, South)  
- Category (Furniture, Office Supplies, Electronics)  
- Product (Laptop, Printer, Table, etc.)  
- Sales  
- Cost  
- Profit  

The dataset was converted into an Excel Table to ensure dynamic updates.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 🔷 KPI Cards
- **Total Revenue:** ₹30,53,783  
- **Total Profit:** ₹6,10,756  
- **Profit Margin:** 20%  
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** 24%  
- **Target Achievement:** 61%  

---

### 🔷 Charts Included

1. Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)  
2. Product-wise Sales (Horizontal Bar Chart)  
3. Regional Sales Comparison (Column Chart)  
4. Category Contribution (Pie Chart)  
5. Sales vs Profit (Combo Chart with Secondary Axis)  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- The company generated ₹30.5L revenue with a healthy 20% profit margin.
- North region is the highest revenue contributor.
- Laptop is the top-performing product.
- Sales target achievement is at 61%, indicating improvement is needed.
- Sales show seasonal fluctuations across months.
- Revenue distribution across categories is balanced.

The dashboard includes slicers for:

All charts dynamically update based on user selection.

## 📌 Conclusion

The Retail Sales Performance Dashboard provides a comprehensive and interactive view of the company’s sales and profitability performance for 2025.

With a total revenue of ₹30.5L and a 20% profit margin, the business demonstrates stable profitability. However, achieving only 61% of the sales target highlights the need for improved sales strategies and focused performance monitoring in the upcoming months.

The dashboard successfully identifies key revenue drivers such as the North region and Laptop product category, while also highlighting seasonal sales fluctuations. The use of dynamic slicers enables management to drill down into specific regions, products, and categories for deeper analysis. 

This dashboard serves as a decision-support tool that helps management monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven strategic decisions.
